Disable a Lens Notification Rule

Disabling a lens notification rule allows you temporarily stop notifications while retaining the logic defined in the notification rule.

  1. Open the lens for which you want to disable a lens notification.
  2. Click Notification rules.
  3. In the Notifications dialog, clear the check box for the notification rule you want to disable.
  4. Click Close.
