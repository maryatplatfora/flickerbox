Why Platfora?

Many organizations deploy Hadoop to store and analyze the massive amounts of data generated by the systems that run their business applications. Platfora™ enables visual, interactive, in-memory data analysis from raw data in Hadoop.

Platfora Reveals Your Data to You

Getting data into Hadoop is relatively easy. The Hadoop file system can ingest virtually any data, structured or unstructured, without the requirement of defining schema ahead of time. This ability allows businesses to store more types of data than ever before.

However, analyzing and gaining insight from data in Hadoop has proven to be more difficult. With so much data, it is difficult to learn the scope of the data itself. Platfora makes the Hadoop data stores accessible by allowing data administrators to catalog their Hadoop data.

Using the Platfora data catalog, business users can explore what data is available. They can discover how "fresh" the data is, who is using it, and what it contains.

Platfora Helps You Use Hadoop Easily and Efficiently

The potential for mining valuable insights from such large Hadoop data stores is huge. Unfortunately, the Hadoop processing and query framework requires advanced programing skills that the typical business user does not have. This skill requirement prevents many organizations from realizing the full power of their Hadoop data.

Platfora encodes Hadoop processing and programming into its software. Users can specify which aspects of the data catalog interests them and request data through Platfora. Platfora translates these user requests into Hadoop processing jobs and returns the results back to the user. This allows users to self-serve their own data requests.

This self-service ensures Hadoop resources are not wasted batch processing and loading data that users don't want or won't use. Platfora pre-aggregates data that is of high-interest across among your users. It stores this data on-disk and refreshes it from your Hadoop stores on a schedule you determine.

Platfora Enables Fast, Up-to-date Business Insights

Once data is in Platfora, you can use Platfora's intuitive, web-based interface to analyze what your data means for your business. Platfora brings data under analysis into memory, this allows users to quickly create sophisticated data visualizations. Users can drag-n-drop data fields, filter for growing trends, identify interesting patterns, and find correlations among data --- all in real time.

Users can group visualizations on dashboards and share these dashboards with others. Platfora data visualizations and the dashboards they appear on automatically update as new data comes. End-to-end Platfora introduces a new level of sophisticated, informed decision making to your business.
