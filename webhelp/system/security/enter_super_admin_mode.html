Entering Super Administrator Mode

System Administrators who have been granted the ability to perform Super Administrator tasks can enter Super Administrator mode.

To enter Super Administrator mode, a user must be assigned the Super Administrator role and be configured to be allowed to perform Super Administrator tasks.

  1. Click your username at the top of the web application.
  2. From the menu that appears, select Switch to Super Administrator.
  3. Enter your password used to log into Platfora.
  4. Click OK.

    Platfora displays a red bar at the top of the web application and changes the user avatar to a flashing red light to make it clear you are currently in Super Administrator mode. You will remain in this mode until you choose to leave it or log out.
