Manage Your Notifications

Platfora tracks and reports the status of certain activities, such as lens builds, and notifies you of status changes. You can view and manage these notifications in your user profile notification list, located in the top-right header of the Platfora application.

Platfora reports on activities per user profile, so you will only be notified on the activities that you initiate or on the objects that you own. When you have a new notification, a blue badge is displayed next to the notification list icon.

For some notification types, you can click the link to open the lens or vizboard that was modified.

  1. Click the notification icon to open your notification list. The blue badge indicates the number of notifications in your current notification list.
  2. For some notification types, you can click the link to open the lens or vizboard that was modified.
  3. Click Clear to clear the notification badge and notification list.
  • Activities that Generate Notifications
    Some activities in Platfora, such as lens builds or data exports, run in the background and can take a while to finish. Other activities, such as comments made in a vizboards, may go unnoticed without some kind of notification. This topic lists the specific activities that are reported in your user profile notification list.
