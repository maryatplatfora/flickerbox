Activities that Generate Notifications

Some activities in Platfora, such as lens builds or data exports, run in the background and can take a while to finish. Other activities, such as comments made in a vizboards, may go unnoticed without some kind of notification. This topic lists the specific activities that are reported in your user profile notification list.
