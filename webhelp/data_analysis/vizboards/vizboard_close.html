Close a Vizboard

To close a vizboard, just navigate off the page by clicking any other page in the top navigation header. Make sure to Save the vizboard before you exit if you do not want to lose your work.

If you try to navigate away from a vizboard page without saving your work first, you will be prompted to either Stay on Page (do not exit without saving first) or Leave Page (exit without saving). If you want to save your work, click Stay on Page. Then click Save before leaving the page again.
