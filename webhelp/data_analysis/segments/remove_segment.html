Remove a Segment from a Vizboard Lens

Removing a segment from a vizboard lens makes it unavailable for analysis. The segment definition and data remain in the Platfora catalog.

You can add the segment to the vizboard lens after you remove it as long as it hasn't been deleted from the dataset.

To remove a segment, click the segment menu in the lens panel for the segment to remove, and click Remove.
