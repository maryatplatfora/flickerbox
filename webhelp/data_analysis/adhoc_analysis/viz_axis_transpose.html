Transpose the X and Y Axis

You can transpose the X and Y axis of a visualization by clicking Swap underneath the X-axis drop zone in the Builder panel. This swaps the fields in the X-axis drop zone with those in the Y-axis drop zone, thereby flipping the orientation of your viz.
