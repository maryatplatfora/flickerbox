Zoom and Pan in a Viz

A visualization (viz) fits into a fixed-size panel on a vizboard page, and is always rendered at 100% scale. To explore different areas of a viz in more detail, you can use the viz pan and zoom controls. The viz view size always re-adjusts to 100% when you change the viz definition, exit the vizboard, or switch between pages.

Zooming In

To zoom in to a particular area of a viz, select the zoom control and click the area of the viz you want to explore. Each click enlarges the viz by 100%.

To enlarge a viz by 100%, click the plus control .

Zooming Out

To zoom out:

  • Select the zoom control , press the CTRL key (on Windows) or Command key (on Mac), and click within the viz.
  • Click the minus control .
  • Click the reset control to reset the viz to 100% size.

Panning

While a viz is zoomed in to larger than 100%, you can use the pan control to drag a particular area of the viz into the view.
